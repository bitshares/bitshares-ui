# Citadel Gateway Service

Citadel is a gateway service built on the Bitshares Exchange. A gateway service is responsible for moving cryptocurrencies to and from the Bitshares Exchange. They support a wide range of popular assets. You can easily identify those supported by Citadel because they are prefixed with the word CITADEL.*. For example CITADEL.MONERO.
